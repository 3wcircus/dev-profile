Baloaner

Baloaner is a Flutter app available on web, Android, and iOS that encourages neighbors to connect by lending tools for specific jobs. Users can open requests for tools, which notify nearby neighbors, and responders can offer tools via private chat to coordinate loans. Open requests are displayed on a Google Map for easy discovery. Leveraging AI tools for scalable backend development, the app fosters community collaboration and reduces the need for one-off purchases.
